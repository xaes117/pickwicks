Find Us

By car :-

From the Oxford Ring Road

Follow the Oxford Ring Road to the roundabout (Green Road) with the A40, then take the A420 into Headington (signposted Headington, Hospitals and Oxford City Centre).

From the A40/M40 (Junction 8)

Follow the road westbound past the Thornhill Park and Ride to the roundabout (Green Road) with the Oxford Ring Road, then go straight across and take the A420 into Headington (signposted Headington, Hospitals and Oxford City Centre).

Pickwick's is located at the junction of London Road with Sandfield Road.

By coach (from London, Heathrow, Gatwick or Stansted) :-

Alight in Headington centre. We are about 300 metres towards Oxford on the right hand side of the road at the junction with Sandfield Road.

By train :-

Either take a taxi or a No. 280 bus from the rail station to Sandfield Road.

Alternatively, take the No. 7, 7c, 8 or 8a bus from Queen Street (opposite Marks and Spencer) to Sandfield Road.
