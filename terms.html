Terms and conditions

All accommodation is offered subject to availability at the time of receipt of a deposit and at the tariff applicable in the current season. To secure a booking either full credit card details including the expiry date and security numbere or a cheque amounting to the first night's accommodation will be required.

Rooms are normally available from 1 pm and must be vacated by 10:30 am on the day of departure. Accounts must be settled in full prior to departure unless alternative arrangements have been made at the time of booking and the proprietor has received written confirmation of this arrangement. Payment may be made by cash or credit card.

In the event of late cancellation or curtailment 48 hours notice must be given otherwise the first night's booking will be charged if the room cannot be relet. Deposits are not refundable but will be deducted from any claim for compensation arising out of any curtailment to the contract.

The proprietor reserves the right to amend, curtail or terminate any contract should the conduct of any person who is a party to that contract causes any detriment to the wellbeing or comfort of other guests.

The proprietor cannot accept responsibility for loss of guests' personal property unless handed in for safe custody.
