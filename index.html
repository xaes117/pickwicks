About Us

We are conveniently situated within five minutes drive from the Oxford ring road and the M40 motorway, and are within walking distance (1½ miles) of Oxford city centre and the University colleges.
There is a frequent bus service just on the doorstep to and from Oxford, and in addition there is a nearby coach stop for 24-hour services to central London, Heathrow, Gatwick and Stansted airports.
