About

Welcome to Pickwick’s, a comfortable, friendly, family-run guest house in Oxford.

We are conveniently situated within five minutes drive from the Oxford ring road and the M40 motorway, and are within walking distance (1½ miles) of Oxford city centre and the University colleges.

There is a frequent bus service just on the doorstep to and from Oxford, and in addition there is a nearby coach stop for 24-hour services to central London, Heathrow, Gatwick and Stansted airports.

We are also in close proximity to Oxford Brookes University, the John Radcliffe, Churchill, Warneford and Manor Hospitals, and the Nuffield Orthopaedic Centre. We provide ample free car parking.

Within easy walking distance are local shops, banks, pubs, restaurants and a post office.

We offer the following facilities :-

  • Single, twin, double and family rooms. Prices vary from £45 to £100 per night.
  • Each room is equipped with a telephone, colour television, radio alarm clock and tea/coffee making facilities
  • Most rooms have en-suite bathrooms or showers with a hair drier.
  • Licensed bar, lounge and large, well-maintained garden for residents’ use.
  • Full English breakfast, or alternative.
  • Free wireless internet access.
